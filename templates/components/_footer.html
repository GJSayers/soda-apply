Connecting Accessibility

Advocating for accessibility being one of our core beliefs as a team, gave us incredible insight allowing us to build this plug-in application. Giving potential candidates and recruiters the opportunity to meet almost seemlessly, and most of all, keeping Ease of Accessibility at the forefront of the recruitment process.

Our team wanted to encompass accessibility too, and not just leave it end with the scope of this web application. Click on the social media icons to get connected with us! And be part of bringing Accessibility to the foregrounds.
