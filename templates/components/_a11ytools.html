Accessibility tools

Below you will find some assistive tools that you can use to better your experience on our site.

Color blindness modes/Font Resizer are the first in a suite of tools that we aim to develop to better help our users have the best possible exposure to a seemless experience. If you have any idea on how we can improve this, please let us know by contacting us via our Contact page.

Our focus on Accessibility is completely dependent on research. So if something in this toolset doesn't make sense to your needs, please let us know. This Accessibility toolset is in beta and is subject to constant change as per modern research.
